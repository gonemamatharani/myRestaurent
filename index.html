FALAFEL

Falafel is a popular Middle Eastern dish made from ground chickpeas or fava beans, mixed with herbs, spices, and onions, and then deep-fried into crispy, flavorful balls or patties. These golden-brown delights are often served in pita bread with tahini sauce, hummus, vegetables, and pickles, making them a delicious and satisfying vegetarian option. Falafel is known for its crunchy exterior and soft, moist interior, offering a delightful contrast of textures with every bite. Enjoyed as a street food or as part of a meal, falafel is a beloved dish celebrated for its rich taste and versatility.

BIRYANI

Biryani is a fragrant and flavorful rice dish that originated in the Indian subcontinent. It is typically made by layering cooked rice with marinated meat (such as chicken, mutton, or beef) and aromatic spices. The dish is then slow-cooked, allowing the flavors to meld together and create a rich and aromatic blend.

KABAB

Kababs are a popular and versatile dish in Middle Eastern, South Asian, and Mediterranean cuisines. They consist of pieces of meat, seafood, or vegetables that are marinated, skewered, and grilled or roasted. Kababs are known for their rich flavors and are often served as appetizers or main courses.Kababs are typically cooked on a skewer over an open flame or grill, which gives them a distinct smoky flavor. They can be made with a variety of meats, including beef, lamb, chicken, and fish, as well as vegetarian options like paneer or mixed vegetables.
